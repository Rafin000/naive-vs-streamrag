# Room Categories and Prices
The resort has 180 rooms in five categories, and every rate includes breakfast:
- Garden View, $120 per night, 28 sqm, up to 2 guests (60 rooms)
- Ocean View, $180 per night, 32 sqm with a balcony, up to 2 guests (60 rooms)
- Family Suite, $260 per night, 55 sqm with two bedrooms, up to 4 guests (40 rooms)
- Beachfront Villa, $450 per night, with a private plunge pool, up to 4 guests (18 villas)
- Presidential Suite, $900 per night, up to 6 guests (2 suites)
