# Restaurants and Bars
Azure Bay Resort has four venues. The Coral Kitchen is the main buffet restaurant. Saltwater
Grill is a beachfront seafood restaurant open 6:00 to 11:00 PM. The Sunset Bar serves cocktails
and light bites from 4:00 PM to midnight. Bean & Leaf Café serves coffee and pastries from 8:00
AM to 6:00 PM.
